Welcome to the team. If you are reading this, you have shown yourself to be a kind, thoughtful, and skilled contributor. Thank you.

## 1. The Philosophy

**"Maintainers are gardeners, not gatekeepers."**

Our job is not to say "No". Our job is to:

1. **Water the plants**: Encourage new contributors.
2. **Prune the vines**: Guide code to fit the vision.
3. **Keep the path clear**: Remove toxicity and confusion.

We optimize for **Contributor Happiness** over Code Perfection.

---

## 3. Decision Making

* **Small Decisions** (Refactors, bug fixes): You have full authority. Merge when ready.
* **Medium Decisions** (New standard library function): Require approval from 1 other Maintainer.
* **Large Decisions** (Syntax changes): Must be proposed as an Issue and approved by the BDFN (Founder).

---

## 4. Handling Conflict

Sometimes things get heated.

1. **De-escalate**: "Let's take a break and come back to this tomorrow."
2. **Private Check-in**: DM the person. Often frustration comes from feeling unheard.
3. **Enforce Boundaries**: If someone violates the Code of Conduct (insults, harassment), warn them immediately. We prioritize safety over code.

---

## 5. Your Health

* **It's okay to step away**: Open Source is a marathon. If you need a week (or month) off, just let us know.
* **No guilt**: You are a volunteer. You owe the project nothing but your kindness.
