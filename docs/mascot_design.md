## 1. Character Bio

* **Name**: "Pump" (or just "The Pumpkin").
* **Role**: Your friendly coding companion.
* **Personality**: Pump is the supportive friend who sits next to you while you code. Pump is never judgmental, never sarcastic, and always calm. Pump loves logic puzzles and seeing humans create things.
* **Vibe**: Think "Baymax" meets "Totoro" but as a small pumpkin. Soft, round, and safe.

---

## 2. Visual Style Guidelines

* **Shape**: Perfectly round or slightly squat pumpkin. Soft edges, no sharp angles.
* **Color**: Vibrant Pumpkin Orange (`#FF7518`) with a vibrant green stem (`#3B8132`).
* **Face**: Simple features. Large, wondering eyes (dots or ovals). A tiny, content smile. No nose.
* **Limbs**: Optional. If present, they are nub-like and minimal (like a plush toy).
* **Texture**: Smooth, matte finish. Not realistic/gritty. 3D rendered style (claymorphism or soft plastic).

---

## 3. Expressions & Use Cases

### üòä Happy (Success)

* **Context**: Code runs successfully, user finishes a lesson.
* **Visual**: Eyes squinting in joy, little sparkles around the head, maybe a small bounce.
* **Message**: "You did it! That was magic."

### ü§î Thinking (Processing)

* **Context**: Code is running, or user is typing.
* **Visual**: One eye slightly larger, stem tilted to the side, looking up.
* **Message**: "Hmm, interesting..."

* **Context**: Syntax error or bug.
* **Visual**: Holding a sign or pointing gently. Concerned but calm eyes. NOT dead/X-eyes.
* **Message**: "Oops! We missed a quote over here. Let's fix it together."

### üéâ Celebrating (Milestone)

* **Context**: First "Hello World", completing a level.

## 4. Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts with tools like Midjourney or DALL-E to generate assets.

**Base Prompt:**
> A cute, minimalist 3D character design of a small round pumpkin mascot. Friendly, large wide eyes, simple happy expression. Soft clay texture, matte finish. Vibrant orange body, bright green stem. Clean solid background. Pixar style, soft lighting, 4k.

**Variations:**

* **Thinking**: `...looking up thoughtfully, stem tilted, questioning expression.`
* **Success**: `...jumping for joy, sparkles, happy squinting eyes, celebratory.`
* **Error Helper**: `...holding a small wrench, helpful expression, pointing gently.`
* **Coding**: `...wearing reading glasses, looking at a computer screen, focused.`
